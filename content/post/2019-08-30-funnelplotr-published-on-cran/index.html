1 FunnelPlotR 0.2.0 released!

I’ve published my package for creating funnel plots of indirectly standardised ratios. It’s awesome! It allows you to make overdispersion adjustments, draws plots and labels outliers in ggplot2.

I will update my post on here to the new function soon, but you can find it at:

  • CRAN: https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/FunnelPlotR/index.html
  • Package Site: https://chrismainey.github.io/FunnelPlotR/
  • GitHub: https://github.com/chrismainey/FunnelPlotR
